Three more women enter Hall of Fame

AMANDA SACHTLEBEN Women entrepreneurship organisation Co. of Women last week inducted three more into its Hall of Fame. The three were insurance pioneer Naomi Ballantyne, retailer Anne Norman and serial entrepreneur Linda Jenkinson. They were chosen from a shortlist of 20, selected by a panel of entrepreneurs, from public nominations.
